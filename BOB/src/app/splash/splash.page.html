What is BOB?

BOB is a platform that allows users to seek help in their respective field. Users can post their queries and other users can answer them. Users can also post their own answers to other users' queries. Users can also upvote/downvote answers and queries.

How to use BOB?

Users can register themselves on the platform and then login to their account. After logging in, users can post their queries and answer other users' queries. Users can also upvote/downvote answers and queries.
